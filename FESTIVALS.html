Karawasan Festival

Karawasan is a festival of ethnic dances depicting the movement of crabs. Karawasan is actually a collective name given to the member of the crab family found to be great in the salty and freshwater of Bislig.

Tinuy-an Sayaw Festival

TINUY-AN SAYAW FESTIVAL. Tinuy-an Sayaw is a theatrical dance that presents the. culture of the Manobo-Mandaya tribe and the history of. Bislig City which all began in the riverbanks of the. majestic Tinuy-an falls.
